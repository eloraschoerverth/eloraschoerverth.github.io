About Me

Hello! I'm Elora, a Computer Science Graduate Student in Berlin, Germany.

The focus during my studies lies on Machine Learning and Distributed Systems. I am especially interested in applying ML in medicine and remote sensing. For my bachelor's thesis I developed a web application supervising the progression of Parkinson's in patients.

Alongside my studies I work at the Fraunhofer HHI Institute, where I am part of AI4H - a focus group building a machine learning assessment platform. The focus group is a cooperation between ITU and WHO, alongside multiple international partners.

Here are a few technologies I've been working with recently:

  • Python
  • Django
  • Cloud Computing
  • Machine Learning
  • React
  • Docker
